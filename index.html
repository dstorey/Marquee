About The Marquee Club

The Marquee Club is a meet-up group in Seattle and the Cascades, for developers and designers who crave for quality in their work. Through our bi-monthly to quarterly meet-ups, we hope to engage in discussion, and promote learning of the latest, most relevant techniques, best practices, and the key tenets of great web design and development. Our guest speakers include some of the most knowledgeable–on the pulse–professionals in the industry; both from the Pacific North-West and beyond.

If you’re interested in developing your skills, and sharing yours with others, in a tolerant, friendly environment, welcome to the Marquee Club. Membership is now open, and free as in beer. [did we mention there will be beer?]
